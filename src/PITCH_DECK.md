# Harvestly Pitch Deck

## Overview
A professional 10-slide pitch deck presentation for Harvestly, India's Farmer Marketplace.

## Slides

1. **Cover Slide** - Brand introduction with tagline
2. **The Problem** - Breaking down India's farm-to-market challenges
3. **Our Solution** - Direct farmer-to-consumer marketplace
4. **How It Works** - 4-step process flow
5. **Business Model** - Revenue streams and monetization
6. **Market Opportunity** - TAM and market statistics
7. **Impact** - Measurable outcomes for farmers and consumers
8. **Why Harvestly?** - Competitive advantages
9. **12-Month Roadmap** - Quarterly growth plan
10. **The Ask** - Investment and partnership request

## Features

- **Harvestly Branding**: Uses brand colors (harvest green), Lora serif for headlines, Manrope sans-serif for body text
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Use ← → arrow keys to navigate between slides
- **Interactive Slide Indicators**: Click any dot to jump to that slide
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for presentations on various screen sizes
- **Clean Layout**: Professional, investor-ready design

## Navigation

- **Next Slide**: Click "Next" button or press → key
- **Previous Slide**: Click "Previous" button or press ← key
- **Jump to Slide**: Click any slide indicator dot
- **Slide Counter**: Shows current slide number at bottom

## Usage

To view the pitch deck, simply run the application. The pitch deck is available as a standalone component that can be presented directly to investors and partners.

## Content Highlights

- **Market Size**: $570B India agriculture market
- **Target**: 50,000 farmers in 12 months across 5 states
- **Impact**: +40% farmer income, 24hr freshness guarantee
- **Partnership**: Ninjacart logistics integration
- **Revenue**: Multiple streams including commission, subscriptions, B2B, and featured listings
